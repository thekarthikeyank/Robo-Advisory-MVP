# Overview

AHAM (Adaptive Hybrid Asset Allocation Model) is a fintech platform developed to automate investor onboarding, behavioral risk assessment, portfolio allocation, investment projections, and client reporting.

The platform combines principles of portfolio management, wealth management, behavioral finance, and workflow automation to generate personalized portfolio recommendations based on investor characteristics and financial objectives.

The project demonstrates how modern financial advisory services can leverage automation and analytics to deliver scalable and personalized investment solutions.

---

# Executive Summary

Traditional investment advisory processes are often manual, time-consuming, and difficult to scale. Financial advisors typically rely on questionnaires, spreadsheets, and subjective assessments when evaluating investor suitability and recommending portfolios.

AHAM addresses these challenges through an automated framework that:

- Evaluates investor risk tolerance
- Generates risk scores
- Classifies investors into portfolio categories
- Produces diversified asset allocations
- Projects future portfolio growth
- Delivers automated investor reports

The objective is to create a consistent, repeatable, and scalable advisory workflow while maintaining a personalized investor experience.

# Core Features

## Investor Risk Profiling

The platform evaluates multiple investor characteristics including:

- Investment Horizon
- Income Stability
- Wealth Allocation
- Risk Behaviour
- Investment Experience
- Investment Goals
- Return Expectations

These variables are used to calculate an overall risk score and investor profile.

---

## Risk Classification

Investors are classified into three categories:

### Conservative

Focus Areas:

- Capital Preservation
- Stable Returns
- Lower Volatility
- Income Generation

### Moderate

Focus Areas:

- Balanced Growth
- Diversification
- Moderate Risk Exposure
- Long-Term Wealth Accumulation

### Aggressive

Focus Areas:

- Wealth Creation
- Higher Equity Allocation
- Long-Term Capital Appreciation
- Higher Return Potential

---

## Portfolio Allocation Framework

The allocation engine constructs diversified portfolios using exposure to:

- NIFTY 50
- Corporate Bonds
- Gold ETFs
- Realty Sector Exposure
- Small Cap Equities
- International Equities (NASDAQ-100)

The asset mix varies according to investor risk profile.

---

## Investment Projection Engine

The system estimates future portfolio values using expected return assumptions and compounding principles.

Projection outputs include:

- Expected Scenario
- Best-Case Scenario
- Base-Case Scenario
- Downside Scenario

These projections help investors understand potential long-term outcomes.

---

## Dashboard & Visualization

The platform includes interactive dashboards displaying:

- Risk Score
- Risk Profile
- Asset Allocation
- Portfolio Composition
- Investment Projections
- Portfolio Analytics

---

## Automated Reporting

The reporting module automatically generates:

- Risk Assessment Summary
- Portfolio Recommendation Report
- Asset Allocation Breakdown
- Investment Growth Projection
- Investor Communication Reports
